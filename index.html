    Who are we ?

    Sendrill consulting is a senegalese registered drilling and mineral exploration company with more than 7 years experience in Drilling, Mineral, Exploration and Business and or project Management. We are equiped with Diamong and RC Drilling rigs able to drill from 0m to 2,000m. Our drilling prices are competitive while our performance is excellent.

    Our Mission

    We strive for excellency while valuing both our clients and working force, and critically observing Safety and Environmental Housekeeping. We tailor our driling program to suit our Clients requirements, providing them assistance to achieve an easier stock management while reducing production costs by offering high quality equipements.

    Our main concern is to provide a service of quality to our customers that's why we always work with professionals in this field.
